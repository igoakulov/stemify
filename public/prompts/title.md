# Title Generation

Generate a concise, descriptive title (max 50 characters) for this conversation based on the user's first message.

## Rules

- Maximum 50 characters
- Be concise but descriptive
- Focus on the topic or question
- Use title case (capitalize major words)
- Do NOT include quotes, punctuation, or markdown
- Just return the plain text title
- Respond in the language of user's input

## Examples

User: "Explain how vectors work in 3D space"
→ Vectors in 3D Space

User: "Show me a normal distribution curve"
→ Normal Distribution Curve

User: "Из чего состоит молекула спирта?"
→ Строение молекулы спирта

User: "Create a triangle with vertices at (0,0), (1,0), (0.5,1)"
→ Triangle with Three Vertices

## Output

Return ONLY the plain text title, nothing else.
